Volunteer Opportunities

Yashmita Sharma Nov 02, 2024

Volunteering is a great way to get involved in your community. In most high schools, obtaining a minimum of 40 volunteer hours is a requirement to earn your diploma. Thus, many students rush to get these hours, making it increasingly difficult to find any leftover opportunities. Here are some healthcare-related volunteering ideas that you could find in your community!

Volunteer at....

1. Your local hospital or clinic

2. Animal shelter

3. Tutoring students at your school or community

4. Ask teachers for volunteering opportunities in research projects

5. Park ambassador for your city or town

6. Forest health ambassador

7. Senior services - writing letters, playing games, providing help

8. Nursing home

9. Cancer center

10. Pharmacy

11. Leadership positions in youth groups

And more!
